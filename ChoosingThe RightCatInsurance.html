Choosing the Right Cat Insurance at the First Instance Itself

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM


      The term insurance on a broader scale covers aspects like illnesses, injuries, accidents, missing cases and death. Same way, pet insurance refers to the blending of these aspects in the context of pets of different species. And in terms of pet insurances, the cat insurance segment is an important segment, on par with dog insurance. The best part about cat insurance is that it’s quite inexpensive. From providing coverage for wellness checkups to illnesses, dental cleanings and covering injury aspects, there are many types of coverage available for cats. Third-party liability insurance is also available.

      Some factors while choosing the right cat insurance are mentioned as below:

  • No 2 cats are similar; choose a cat insurance taking into consideration the cat’s special needs like hereditary conditions, breed based issues and other genetic factors.
  • The golden rule here is: consult your doctor before going for any pet insurance.
  • Getting insurance quotes from multiple cat insurance providers will put you on a better platform while choosing the most appropriate insurance.
  • Check for any feedback about the insurance providers from friends and also over the internet. In the feedback, specially look for any small clauses which get exploited like say, front and back declawing.
  • For young cats, choose an insurance concentrating more on injuries than illnesses and vice versa for older cats because cats of different ages are vulnerable to different types of discomforts.

     Lastly, it is recommended to check all the terms and conditions well in advance prior to you signing the dotted line.
